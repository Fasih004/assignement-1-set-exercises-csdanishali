Spicy Garlic Shrimp Pasta

This Spicy Garlic Shrimp Pasta is a quick and flavorful dish that combines succulent shrimp with a spicy garlic sauce, all tossed in al dente pasta. Perfect for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion!

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti or linguine)
  • 300g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp red chili flakes
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Method

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and chili flakes, saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the shrimp to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until pink and cooked through.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta into the pan with the shrimp. Add salt, pepper, and Parmesan cheese. Mix well.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot. Enjoy!
