Overview

Outer Banks is a Netflix original adventure series set in the coastal Outer Banks of North Carolina. It follows the Pogues — a group of working-class teens — as they uncover clues to a legendary treasure tied to John B’s missing father.

What starts as a summer quest turns into a dangerous journey full of rivalries, romance, and betrayal as the Pogues clash with the elite Kooks and the law. At the heart of it all is friendship, loyalty, and the fight for something bigger than themselves.

Cast

  • Chase Stokes as John B
  • Madelyn Cline as Sarah Cameron
  • Madison Bailey as Kiara
  • Rudy Pankow as JJ
  • Jonathan Daviss as Pope

Characters

  • John B: The fearless leader chasing treasure and the truth about his dad.
  • Sarah Cameron: A Kook-turned-Pogue who finds herself at odds with her family.
  • JJ: The wild card, impulsive but fiercely loyal to his friends.
  • Kiara: Rich but rebellious, she defies expectations to stand by the Pogues.
  • Pope: The smart, strategic one with a scholarship dream — and a stake in the treasure.

The Treasure Hunt

The story kicks off when John B discovers a clue to the Royal Merchant — a shipwreck rumored to hold $400 million in gold. What follows is a race against time, rivals, and corrupt powers who will do anything to claim it first.

As the Pogues get deeper into the mystery, they uncover long-buried secrets and form unbreakable bonds — all under the blazing Outer Banks sun.
