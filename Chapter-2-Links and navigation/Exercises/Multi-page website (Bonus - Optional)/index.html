Welcome to Outer Banks

Outer Banks is a high-energy teen adventure drama set in the coastal Carolinas. Follow the Pogues, a tight-knit group of working-class teens, as they uncover long-lost secrets and hunt for treasure while navigating love, danger, and betrayal.

When John B’s dad disappears, the crew sets out on a life-changing journey that pits them against the elite “Kooks,” law enforcement, and the lawless world of buried treasure. The stakes are high, and paradise is full of secrets.
