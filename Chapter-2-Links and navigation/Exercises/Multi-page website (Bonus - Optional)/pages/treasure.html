The Treasure

The treasure in Outer Banks is the legendary Royal Merchant gold, a massive fortune lost at sea centuries ago. John B believes this gold is connected to his father's disappearance.

The search for the gold propels the group into dangerous territory as they uncover lies, family secrets, and a much deeper conspiracy. It’s not just about the riches — it’s about legacy, truth, and survival.
