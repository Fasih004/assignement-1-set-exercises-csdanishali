Plot

Outer Banks follows a group of working-class teens, the Pogues, living on the sunny coast of North Carolina. Their lives take a thrilling turn when they discover clues to a legendary treasure linked to John B’s missing father.

As the Pogues race against the elite Kooks and law enforcement, they uncover secrets, face betrayals, and navigate growing romantic tensions — all while trying to stay one step ahead in their treasure hunt. Adventure, mystery, and danger collide in this high-stakes series full of twists and action.
